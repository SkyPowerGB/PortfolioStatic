Finally

The long-awaited portfolio site is finally finished. It has been functional for quite some time, but I was holding off on the public release until I completed a full content update.

This is the space where I will publish my projects and document my ongoing development across all fronts. The line between projects and hobbies can get a bit blurry at times, but that's part of the fun. Technically, this site is a project in itself; I built a custom JS-based content loading system designed to manage and append content within a static site architecture relatively easily. I opted for a static site approach primarily for security and performance reasons.

In the "Projects" section, I will focus on coding-related work, while "Hobbies" will cover everything else or serve as a personal showcase.
