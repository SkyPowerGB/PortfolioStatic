Fish feeder

Its just simple contraption i put i made in limited time of 3 days to feed fishes while we re gone for 5 days ,

Initial idea was to do it arduinoless. It simply makes no sense to use microcontroller for such simple task. Idea was to have DC motor drive the wheel while the LDR + LED will detect position . Motor would been driven by transistor which gets controlled trough LDR and resistor voltage divider and should trun off when the hole is detected. Next thing would be to generate 24H pulse or use smart home wifi controlled relay.

Design was made in an oversized manner , i didnt had low rpm dc motor. Issue is if motor has too much momentum detection probably wont work or motor might just skip hole. Without time to desing proper gearbox and lack of percision on 3d printer, i went for belt drive plus aditionally hoped to use some friction to slow it down. End result was that wheel still span way too quick and it had 0 friction so idea was srachted and imidetly went to reuse parts of desing while this time have stepper drive it. The rubber belt had issues of skiping steps so the LDR LED circuit part proved to be useful and working as itended.

After writing arduino code that moves stepper every 24h to next hole, the contraption was fueled and left. In end after returning home, we found out that USB plug into which it was plugged had play inside and device lost power.

Conclusion

In end project was boht succes and failure. Overall model desing is terrible and oversized so whole contraption was scrapped . Some time in near future i might make redesign and this time with proper care and even including arduinoless circuit. Issue with old desing is bad interface as well as too big plus wasting ton of plastics for no reason.
